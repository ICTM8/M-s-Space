About Us

Margarett's Space is a website originally made as a homework in Margarett's ICT Class. This website is purely made to show off cute images of the superior organism (hamsters). This website was made with blood, sweat, tears, and a bunch of W3 Schools Tutorials (and old Reddit posts). In the making of this website, Margarett had to learn a lot of new tags and terms such as; "Display", "z-index", "max/min-width/height", the use of box shadows, "gap", "line-height", "aligning items vertically/horizontally", "how to add an area where users can enter data", rgba (red,green,blue,ALPHA?!", and her new personal favorite borders, outset and inset. But, through thorough research and a thousand tabs, she got through it.
